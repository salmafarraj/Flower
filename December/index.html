December

Narcissus (Paperwhites):
Paperwhites symbolize hope, renewal, and self-reflection, often blooming indoors during winter. Fun fact: they’re part of the daffodil family and can grow without soil using just water and pebbles.

Holly:
Holly represents protection and domestic happiness, with its shiny green leaves and bright red berries. Fun fact: it was considered sacred by the Druids and used to ward off evil spirits during the winter solstice.
