November

Chrysanthemum:
Chrysanthemums symbolize cheerfulness, loyalty, and longevity, especially popular in East Asian cultures. Fun fact: In Japan, there’s a “Festival of Happiness” each year devoted to this flower.

Peony:
Peonies represent romance, prosperity, and good fortune, with lush, fragrant blooms. Fun fact: In Chinese culture, the peony is considered the "king of flowers" and symbolizes wealth and honor.
