Larkspur
Larkspur symbolizes an open heart, strong bonds, and positivity, with tall spikes of vibrant flowers. Fun fact: each color of larkspur has its own meaning—pink for fickleness, white for joy, and purple for first love.

Water Lily :
Water lilies represent purity, enlightenment, and peace, often floating serenely on still ponds. Fun fact: they were sacred in ancient Egypt, symbolizing rebirth as they bloom at sunrise and close at sunset.
