Rose
Roses are timeless symbols of love, passion, and beauty, with each color expressing a different emotion. Fun fact: the rose is the national flower of the United States and England.

Honeysuckle:
Honeysuckle signifies devoted affection and the bonds of love with its sweet scent and clinging vines. Fun fact: hummingbirds are especially drawn to its nectar-rich flowers.
