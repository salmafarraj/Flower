Febuary

Violet
Violets stand for modesty, faithfulness, and humility, with their heart-shaped leaves and delicate blooms. Fun fact: Napoleon Bonaparte used violets to symbolize his loyalty to his wife Josephine.

Primrose:
Primroses symbolize young love and the feeling of "I can't live without you." Fun fact: the name comes from *prima rosa*, Latin for "first rose," as they are among the earliest spring bloomers., it’s traditional to wear a daffodil on St. David’s Day.
