Daisy
Daisies represent innocence, purity, and cheerfulness with their sunny, simple appearance. Fun fact: each daisy is actually two flowers in one—the central disc and the outer petals.

Sweet Pea :
Sweet peas convey blissful pleasure and goodbye, often given at the end of visits. Fun fact: they were a favorite flower of the Edwardian era and are still popular in weddings today.
