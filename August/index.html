August

Gladiolus:
Gladiolus stands for strength, integrity, and remembrance, and its tall stems evoke the image of a sword. Fun fact: Gladiolus was once worn by Roman gladiators as a charm for protection in battle.

Poppy
Poppies symbolize imagination, sleep, and remembrance, particularly honoring fallen soldiers in many countries. Fun fact: Red poppies are used for Remembrance Day in Commonwealth nations.
