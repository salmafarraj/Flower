January

Carnation
Carnations symbolize love, fascination, and admiration, blooming in many colors with rich meanings. Fun fact: pink carnations are thought to have first grown from the Virgin Mary’s tears, making them a symbol of motherly love.

snowdrop:
Snowdrops represent hope and rebirth, often pushing through frozen ground to bloom in late winter. Fun fact: they were once considered unlucky because of their association with death, but now they’re seen as signs of renewal.
