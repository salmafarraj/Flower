October

Marigold:
Marigolds are associated with creativity, passion, and remembrance, especially in vibrant orange and gold hues. Fun fact: They're used heavily in Día de los Muertos celebrations in Mexico to honor the dead.

Cosmos:
Cosmos symbolizes harmony, peace, and order, with symmetrical petals that inspired their name from the Greek word for "order." Fun fact: They’re one of the easiest flowers to grow from seed, thriving even in poor soil.
